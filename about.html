Our Mission Statement

We at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham took the initiative to save cows that are injured and unhealthy. The moment when a cow grows old and stops producing milk, its owner disowns them.They think that the cow is useless. Many cows are left abandoned and neglected on the roads because of such owners. Some cows meet with an accident, get injured, are infected with bacteria and pus, and are left to die. At the same time, some others die due to old age or get captured by butchers in the city.Hence, living a miserable life waiting for their death.

So we at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham want to save these cows and give them a better life. We bring injured and unhealthy cows to our gaushalas in their worst condition, such as with injuries, disabled, or suffering from other diseases. We even protect cows that are illegally taken away to butcher houses to get killed.In our ashram, all these cows are fed regularly to gain health; we provide shelter and look after buying our volunteers or disciples. We keep and a keen eye on their health 24*7. We even make sure that a vet gives them a regular visit to ensure that the cows in our gaushalas are healthy and fit.Once they are declared healthy and can produce milk, these cows are given as a danam to widows in rural areas who barely have any means to survive in their life.We encourage and want people to adopt cows in our gaushala and look after them by providing a certain amount to look after the cow.
