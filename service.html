Goushal

We at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham took the initiative to save cows are left abandoned and neglected on the roads because of such owners. Some cows meet with an accident, get injured, are infected with bacteria and pus, and are left to die. At the same time, some others die due to old age or get captured by butchers in the city.Hence, living a miserable life waiting for their death.

So we at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham want to save these cows and give them a better life. We bring injured and unhealthy cows to our gaushalas in their worst condition, such as with injuries, disabled, or suffering from other diseases. We even protect cows that are illegally taken away to butcher houses to get killed.

In our ashram, all these cows are fed regularly to gain health; we provide shelter and look after buying our volunteers or disciples. We keep and a keen eye on their health 24*7. We even make sure that a vet gives them a regular visit to ensure that the cows in our gaushalas are healthy and fit. We encourage people to adopt cows in our gaushala and look after them by providing a certain amount to look after the cow. If you are interested in adopting a cow and being a part of our family

Feed pregnant Mother

Proper nutrition during pregnancy is crucial for mothers; it is also vital to the health of their infants. Poor nutrition puts pregnant mothers and their unborn babies at potential risk of disease and death.Malnutrition is a significant concern for pregnant women in rural, tribal, and economically backward areas. Inadequate energy supply to the mother's body during pregnancy results in low infant birth weight.

We at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham have started the initiative to feed pregnant women in social and economically backward areas even before the government has started the initiative in INDIA.

We provide nutritious food to pregnant women, including dairy products, vegetables, foods rich in Folic acid, calcium, vitamin D,and others.If you want to donate or want to be a part of the mission.

Anna Daanam

“Annad Bhavani Bhutani” In Srimad Bhagavad Gita, Lord Sri Krishna mentioned that from food, all beings evolve, and food becomes one of the essential criteria to survive.Anna Danam is one of the best forms of danam in Sanatan Dharmam.  Anna Danam, in holy scriptures like Ramayana and Mahabharat, considered feeding the poor (Anna Danam) as a more effective form of service.

>At Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham, We consider this an essential part of duty and arrange Annadanam for the needy and poor. At our Asharam, we organize Annadanam five times a month on a once-weekly basis. However, we prioritize doing annadanam to roadside dwellers, mentally unstable, and homeless people.we are planning to upgrade it to a Nitya Anndanam, i.e., donating food daily.Even you can donate food and do this great seva to humanity. In addition, you can donate money to do annadanam on your birthdays, anniversaries, and on

Das Sahasra Sree Rama Koti Japa Mahayagnam.

The main aim of conducting this Mahayagnam is to create interest for people swaying away from our Sanatana Dharma and perform Nitya Agni Hotram (A yagnam that goes on for 3 hrs). This program is organized in extreme rural and tribal areas that are completely cut off from the city and are surrounded by hills.

We at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham are preaching to them the importance of our dharma and enlightening them with the stories about Lord Rama, teaching them recitals, and explaining Sanatan Dharma. We carry all the necessary items for puja such as Kashaya Jhanda, Image of Ram,   …….. with ourselves and make the rural and tribal people participate in Pujas to create interest in our Sanatana Dharma.

Under this initiative, we construct or help construct temples by providing comments, bricks, and funds for building their gram devata temple in rural and tribal areas. To date, we have donated more than 100 idols to temples.

Veda pathashala

Abhyasam kos vidya is an initiative taken by Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham, to teach Veda adhyayam to kids. These classes will help kids have more interest in the rich cultural history of their ancestors and how to live life, according to Sanatan Dharmam.

Kids and teenagers in these Ved adhyayam classes are given Srimad Bhagavad Gita classes every Sunday from 8 to 11.30. Along with Gita classes, girls learn Stree Soktam, while boys learn Purus Soktam. In addition, we provide free tuition classes to poor kids at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham.After classes are over, the kids are provided lunch in the afternoon as a part of our program. Apart from that, self-defense classes are provided for girls, including the usage of sticks, martial arts, and others.

Water Support

We provide water support to socially backward remote rural areas which are entirely cut-off from the city and have no proper facility to get drinking water. To get drinking water, people from these areas have to walk a minimum of 4km - 5km carrying water containers.Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham took the initiative to provide drinking water to these areas.


Homeless Support

Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham takes care of homeless people by providing them food, blankets, clothes, and other support.


Tree Plantation

“Trees are considered an important part of our life as it is the sole producer of oxygen on earth for free. However, due to huge deforestation there has been decrease in the level of fresh oxygen. Trees plays an important role to keep the soil clean, prevent heavy damages from flooding and makes earth a beautiful place to live.

We at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham encourage people to plant trees especially Neem tree, Banyan Tree and Bael tree (these are known for their health and medicinal value) along with other trees. We organise tree plantation events where our volunteers along with people who are interested in planting tress gather together and plant trees and pledge to save our trees.
