SRI SAI DATTA MANASA PEETHAM

We at Sri Sai Datta Manasa Peetham took the initiative to save cows that are injured and unhealthy. The moment when a cow grows old and stops producing milk, its owner disowns them.They think that the cow is useless. Many cows are left abandoned and neglected on the roads because of such owners. Some cows meet with an accident, get injured, are infected with bacteria and pus, and are left to die. At the same time, some others die due to old age or get captured by butchers in the city.Hence, living a miserable life waiting for their death.
